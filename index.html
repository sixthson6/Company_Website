Home

Welcom to MilkyWorld, a plant-based milk company committed to providing the best dairy alternatives for you.

Flagship Product

Our flagship product, Millet Delight, is a rich, creamy, and irresistible plant-based milk made from the finest millet grains.

Lactose Intolerance

Millet Delight is specially formulated for the lactose intolerant population, providing a rich source of proteins, calcium, iron and other essential nutrients.

Millet, a Super Food

Millet is a superfood that is rich in proteins, fiber, and essential nutrients. It is also gluten-free and has a low glycemic index.

Mission & Vission

Mission & Vision

Mission Statement

To revolutionize dairy alternatives by producing high-quality millet-based milk and plant-based products that cater to lactose-intolerant individuals, vegans, and health-conscious consumers, while supporting local agriculture, reducing our environmental footprint, and fostering community well-being.

Vision Statement

To be the leading provider of sustainable, plant-based milk solutions that empower healthier lifestyles, address dietary challenges, and promote environmental sustainability across Africa and the world.

Impact

MilkyWorld's Impact

Job Creation
MilkyWorld has created over 100 direct and indirect jobs in the local community, providing employment opportunities for farmers, processors, distributors, and other stakeholders in the value chain.
Empowering Smallholder Farmers
MilkyWorld sources its millet grains from smallholder farmers in rural communities, providing them with a stable source of income and access to new markets for their produce.
Positive Impact on the Climate
By promoting millet cultivation, MilkyWorld is contributing to climate change mitigation by sequestering carbon in the soil, reducing greenhouse gas emissions, and promoting sustainable agricultural practices.
